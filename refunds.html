⚠️ No-Refund Policy: All Zayvora Engine credit purchases are final and non-refundable.

1. Policy Statement

Zayvora Engine operates on a no-refund policy for all credit purchases. Once credits are purchased and added to your account, they cannot be refunded or exchanged for money under any circumstances.

2. Credit Characteristics

Credits are non-refundable digital tokens with the following characteristics:

  • Non-transferable between accounts
  • No monetary value outside the platform
  • Cannot be exchanged for cash
  • No expiration date - valid indefinitely
  • Subject to platform terms of service

3. Exceptions (Limited)

The only exceptions to this no-refund policy are:

  • Duplicate Charges: If you are charged twice for the same purchase due to a system error, we will refund the duplicate charge within 7 business days of verification.
  • Fraudulent Transactions: If your account was compromised and unauthorized purchases were made, contact us immediately at dharam@viadecide.com with proof.
  • Payment Failure: If your credit card or UPI payment failed but credits were still added, we will investigate and refund if verified as an error.

4. Dispute Resolution Process

If you believe you qualify for an exception, follow these steps:

  • Contact us within 48 hours at dharam@viadecide.com
  • Provide a detailed description of the issue
  • Include transaction ID and screenshots if possible
  • We will review and respond within 5 business days
  • If approved, refund will be processed to original payment method within 7 business days

5. Failed Transactions

If your payment was not successful, credits should not be added to your account. If you were charged but did not receive credits, please contact support immediately with your transaction details. We will investigate and resolve within 24 hours.

6. Account Termination

If your account is terminated due to violation of our Terms of Service, any remaining credits will be forfeited and non-refundable. This includes accounts terminated for:

  • Unauthorized access attempts
  • Fraudulent activity
  • Violation of acceptable use policy
  • Abusive or illegal behavior

7. Changes to Policy

We reserve the right to modify this Refund Policy at any time. Modifications apply only to future purchases. All purchases made prior to policy changes are governed by the policy in effect at the time of purchase.

8. Why No-Refund?

Our no-refund policy exists because:

  • Credits are delivered immediately and used instantly
  • Preventing fraud and account abuse
  • Credits never expire and remain permanently on your account
  • We offer competitive pricing and regular promotional offers
  • Keeping costs low for all users

9. Contact Support

For refund inquiries or disputes, contact us at: dharam@viadecide.com

Please include your account email, transaction ID, and a detailed description of your request.
