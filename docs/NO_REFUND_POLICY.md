**ALL ZAYVORA AI CREDIT PURCHASES ARE FINAL AND NON-REFUNDABLE.**

## 1. SCOPE OF NO-REFUND POLICY

### 1.1 Non-Refundable Circumstances
Refunds will NOT be issued for:

- Change of mind or service dissatisfaction
- Inability to access Zayvora due to connectivity issues
- Browser or device compatibility issues
- Accidental credit purchases
- Account suspension for Terms violation
- Account deletion with unused credits
- Currency fluctuations affecting perceived value
- Perceived poor response quality from the LLM

## 2. EXCEPTIONS TO NO-REFUND POLICY

Credits will ONLY be reissued in these narrow circumstances:

### 2.1 Zayvora Infrastructure Failure
**Scenario:** Zayvora service is completely unavailable for more than 24 consecutive hours.

**Requirements:**
- Service was completely inaccessible
- Downtime exceeded 24 hours continuously
- Support ticket filed within 48 hours
- Technical evidence provided

**Resolution:** Credits reissued to account (not cash).

### 2.2 Duplicate Billing by Payment Processor
**Scenario:** PhonePe charged you multiple times for a single purchase.

**Requirements:**
- PhonePe transaction receipts showing duplicate charges
- Exact transaction IDs and amounts
- Bank statement proof of multiple debits
- Support ticket filed within 48 hours

**Resolution:** Duplicate amount credited to account or refunded via PhonePe.

### 2.3 Fraudulent or Unauthorized Transaction
**Scenario:** Someone else made a credit purchase using your account without authorization.

**Requirements:**
- Report submitted within 7 days of unauthorized purchase
- Proof that account was compromised
- Password change proof
- Detailed explanation of unauthorized access

**Resolution:** Unauthorized credits deducted from account. User is responsible for account security.

## 4. COMPLIANCE WITH CONSUMER PROTECTION LAWS

### 4.1 Indian Consumer Protection Act, 2019
This policy complies with Section 19(1) of the CPA, 2019, which permits no-refund policies for digital services where:
- The service is provided immediately upon purchase
- The service is immediately usable
- Consumption is initiated by the consumer

For digital services, refunds for "change of mind" are explicitly excluded.

### 4.2 GST Compliance
All credit purchases include 18% GST as per Indian tax law:
- ₹50 base = ₹59 with GST (50 credits)
- ₹100 base = ₹118 with GST (120 credits)

GST-included amounts are non-refundable.

### 4.3 RBI Regulations
- PhonePe processes all payments under RBI supervision
- Zayvora has no authority to override PhonePe's refund decisions
- Disputes must be filed with PhonePe directly

You have the right to escalate under Indian consumer protection laws:

**Step 1: Email Appeal**
- Email legal@daxini.xyz with detailed appeal
- Wait 30 days for response

**Step 2: RBI Complaint**

- Category: "Payment Gateway/Processor Grievance"
- Include: Transaction ID, denial letter, evidence

- File with District Consumer Commission
- Jurisdiction: Kutch District, Gujarat
